ABAF President: Dobromir Karamarinov / BUL (President Bulgarian Athletic Federation)






Curriculum Vitae:

  • Name: KARAMARINOV
  • First name: DOBROMIR
  • Date of birth: 26 March 1958
  • Nationality: Bulgarian
  • Profession: Track and Field coach
  • Spoken Languages: Bulgarian, English, Russian

Present Occupation:

  • since 2004 - President of Bulgarian Athletic Federation

Occupied positions:

  • since Oct. 2010 - President of the Association of the Balkan Athletics Federations (ABAF)
  • since Aug. 2007 - Member of the IAAF Women’s Committee
  • since Apr. 2007 - European Athletics Council member
  • 2004 – 2007 - Member of the EAA Reflection Commission
  • 1995-2003 - Member of the EAA Competition Committee
  • since 1994 ­ European Athletics Representative and Jury of Appeal at numerous International Meetings
  • Meeting Director of multiple International Meetings
  • Team Leader of the National Teams at all European and World Championships
  • Delegate at all European Athletics and IAAF Congresses
  • Lecturer at the National Sports Academy

Working Experience:

  • 1994-2004 - General Secretary of Bulgarian Athletic Federation
  • 1993-1994 - Responsible for Olympic preparation in the Federation
  • 1991-1994 - Head Coach in Athletics
  • 1985-1990 - President of Yantra Gabrovo Sports Club

Active Sport Career:

  • 1972-1982 - 400mH Balkan and National Champion many times

Educational Background:

  • 1978-1982 - National Sports Academy
  • 1972-1976 - National School of Mathematics
