Fatih AVAN (TUR) –Javelin Throw 84.79m (NR) in 2011 Gold medallist at World University Games, Shenzhen 2011 Gold medallist U23 at EC Winter Throwing, Sofia 2011 Silver medallist at European U23 Championships, Ostrava 2011 5th at World Championships, Daegu 2011



Krasimir BRAYKOV (BUL) – 400m (46.09) First place at the European Team Championships, Novi Sad 2011 Balkan Champion, Sliven 2011



Dimitrios CHONDROKOUKIS (GRE) – High Jump Dimitris is one of the most promising young athletes with a personal best of 2m32, performance standing among the best in Men’s High Jump, worldwide. He was placed 5th in the IAAF World Championships in Daegu, 1st in the European Team Championships in Izmir (18/06) and 1st in the IAAF Weltklasse Meeting in Zurich (Diamond League on 08/09), not to forget that he is the 2011 National Champion. He belongs to Olympiakos Athletic Club.



Mihail DUDAS (SRB) – Decathlon 6th place (8256 points) at the IAAF World Championships, Daegu 2011 3rd place at the European U23 Championships/Ostrava Decathlon



Ion LUCHIANOV (MDA) - 3000m Steeplechase 8th place at the IAAF World Championships, Daegu 2011.



Marian Oprea (ROU) – Triple Jump Bronze medal at the European Indoor Championships, Paris 2011- 17,62 m
