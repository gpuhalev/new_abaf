ABAF Flag Raised in Istanbul


With ten days to go to the first Balkan event of the year, the ABAF flag is raised in front of Istanbul's Atakoy Athletics Arena. A brief ceremony was held with the attendance of Emel Dereli, Balkan Rising Star 2013 and her coach Ihsan Ozden. The Ceremony was hosted by Can Korkmazoglu, Istanbul Branch Director of the Turkish Athletic Federation.



Istanbul will host the Balkan Indoor Championships for the third consecutive year on 22 February 2014. The Championships will be historical as all fifteen Member Federations are expected to gather for the first time. Cyprus, Georgia and Slovenia were affiliated to ABAF during the regular Congress held in October in Tallinn last year.



Following the hoisting of the ABAF flag, Emel Dereli, World Youth and European Junior champion last year, mentioned that the ABAF events are of special value to her as she gained her first international title in a Balkan event. Dereli, who also holds the European Indoor Junior Record since December, promised to do her utmost in the Balkan Indoor Championships.

The Ceremony was concluded after Can Korkmazoglu conveyed a message from the President of the Turkish Athletic Federation, Mr. Fatih Cintimar, who extended his best wishes to the ABAF Members.



The ABAF flag will fly on the pole until the end of the Championships among the flags of Turkey, the Ministry of Youth and Sports of Turkey and the Turkish Athletic Federation.
