ABAF General Secretary: Galia Puhaleva / BUL (International Secretary Bulgarian Athletic Federation)






Curriculum Vitae:

  • Name: PUHALEVA
  • First name: Galia
  • Date of birth: 29 June 1974
  • Nationality: Bulgarian
  • Spoken Languages: Bulgarian, English, Franch, Russian

Working Experience:

  • Since Oct 2010 - General Secretary of the Association of the Balkan Athletics Federations (ABAF)
  • 2010 - Event Coordinator and Key person for the organisation of the European Mountain Running Championships, Sapareva Banya / BUL, 4 July 2010
  • Since Sept 2009 - Council Member of the World Mountain Running Association (WMRA)
  • Since Apr 2009 - Member of the European Athletics Reflection Commission
  • Since 2005 - Bulgarian Athletic Federation, International Secretary;
  • Organizational Manager for a number of International athletics competitions, such as the Balkan Mountain running Championships 2008 & 2009, Balkan Championships 2007, Balkan Cross Country Championships 2007, European Athletics Calendar Conference 2006, Mountain Running Seminar 2009
  • Team Leader of Bulgarian National teams for a number of international competitions including European and World Championships
  • IT Services, FinishLynx Timing system, Photo finish and False start during athletic competitions
  • 2003 - IT Services Eurohockey Club Champions Challenge Men
  • 1995–1999: Bulgarian Volleyball Federation, International relations. Organizational Manager for several editions of the World Volleyball League (home matches)

Education:

  • 2002 - Postgraduate qualification Basic Passenger Fares and Ticketing, Travel agent, Training centre Bulair, IATA
  • 1997 - Sofia University, Faculty of Slavic Philology; major subject Bulgarian language and literature specialist, High school teacher
  • 1995 - College of Journalism (correspondence courses); major subject Radio Programme Director
  • 1993 - Postgraduate qualification Tourist guide with French and English,
  • 1992 - French Lyceum in Sofia, French language and literature,
